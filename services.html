Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and the timeline for seeing results can vary depending on factors like your industry competitiveness, website age, and current optimization level. Typically, you may start seeing some improvements in 3-6 months, with more significant results appearing after 6-12 months of consistent optimization. We provide monthly reports to track progress and make adjustments as needed.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving your website's organic (unpaid) search rankings through various optimization techniques. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads that appear at the top of search results. While SEO provides long-term, sustainable growth, PPC offers immediate visibility and traffic. We often recommend a combination of both for maximum impact.

How do you measure the success of social media campaigns?

We track a variety of metrics depending on your campaign goals, including engagement rate (likes, comments, shares), follower growth, website traffic from social, lead generation, and conversion rates. We establish clear KPIs before launching any campaign and provide detailed monthly reports showing progress toward these goals.

Do you work with businesses in any industry?

Yes, we've worked with clients across various industries including e-commerce, healthcare, professional services, technology, and more. Each industry requires a slightly different approach, and we tailor our strategies accordingly. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your specific industry challenges and opportunities.

What's included in your monthly reports?

Our comprehensive monthly reports include key performance metrics, progress toward goals, insights into what's working and what needs adjustment, and recommendations for the coming month. For SEO, this includes rankings, traffic, and backlink data. For PPC, we provide conversion rates, cost-per-lead, and ROI analysis. Social media reports show engagement metrics and audience growth. We're happy to customize reports to focus on the metrics that matter most to you.
